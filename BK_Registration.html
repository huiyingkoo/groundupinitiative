Farming the land farms our hearts as well

Farming is all about being connected with the ground and with nature. We plant herbs, we expose ourselves to the medicinal wonders of plants, we look after our terrace of amazing greens and we become so close to the land that we smell of earth. Through that, we maintain a sense of groundedness and humility, farming the land to grow as well as ourselves to cultivate our hearts and our minds.
